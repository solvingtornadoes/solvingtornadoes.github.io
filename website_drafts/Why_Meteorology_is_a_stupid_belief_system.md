Why Meteorology is a stupid belief system pretending to be an intelligent scientific model.  
The culprit is fundamental assumptions that appear sensible but that are actually nonsense.  
And it is the desire to keep models simple so that they can be easily conveyed to subsequent generations.  
Specifically we are talking about water and the tendency to see water as something it isn’t and to dismiss observations that indicate  that water is different than we believe.
And so, meteorology is stupid because of human emotions that cause us to dismiss the behavioral complexity of water inducing us to assume behaviors of water that are plainly stupid.
The purpose of this examination is to expose the circle of stupidity that causes us to misunderstand the physics of storms.
